Powerful Features

AI Emotion Detection

Our advanced AI analyzes your facial expressions or biometric data in real-time to accurately determine your emotional state.

Biometric Sensing

Connect a heart rate sensor to generate playlists based on your live BPM and HRV data for a deeper connection.

Dynamic Playlists

How It Works

Getting started with AuraTune is simple and intuitive

1

Sign Up

Create your free AuraTune account to unlock your personalized dashboard and save your preferences.

2

Choose Your Method

Select your input: Use your camera for facial analysis or connect a sensor for a vitals-based experience.

3

Enjoy Music

Sit back and enjoy music that perfectly matches your current emotional state, automatically.

Your Privacy is Our Priority: All emotion analysis is done in real-time on your device. We never store or transmit your camera footage.

About Us

The final year team behind AuraTune

Final Year Project Team

Team Members: Jazeera, Rishana, Ihsan, Anwarsha

"As final year students passionate about the intersection of AI and user experience, we created AuraTune to explore how technology could connect more deeply with human emotion. This project is the culmination of our collaborative effort to build a smarter, more intuitive music player. Our goal was to design an application that doesn't just play songs, but listens back, creating a truly personalized soundtrack for any mood. We're proud to present AuraTune as our final year project."
